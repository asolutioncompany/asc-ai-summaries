#aS.c AI Summaries

WordPress Plugin to automatically generate, add, and style AI summaries for your WordPress content

## Pre-Production / Early Development

This plugin is not ready for production use and is in early development.

This plugin is being developed for public use as Free and Open Source Software.

The initial commit includes administrative options and meta panel.

It does not yet automatically generate AI summaries, style and add them to your WordPress content, nor connect to AI models.

## Planned Features

Automatically generate AI summaries for all your posts and a variety of customizable styles.

- Improves SEO.

- Helps busy people quickly read about your content.

- Setup different AI models for automatic generation.

- Check and automatically generate summaries for all previously published WordPress content.

- Automatically generate and edit short and long summaries when editing WordPress content.

- Automatically generate WordPress excerpts from your short summary.

- Select and customize a variety of styles to automatically show your summaries on your WordPress content.
